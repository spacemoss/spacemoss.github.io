About

Aquaduck is an independent literary magazine in Oxford, Ohio. It features content of a unique, expiremental, or non-traditional form. Aquaduck accepts text, art, photography, video and sound of all creative natures. It was founded in 2008 by Miami University students: Alex Dannemiller, Pierce Denny, and Rachel Schramm.
