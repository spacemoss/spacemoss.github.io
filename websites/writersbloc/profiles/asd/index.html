Alex Dannemiller

Born in Cincinnati, Ohio and currently studying Creative Writing at Miami University. Alex mainly writes short (and flash) fiction ranging from sci-fi to horror and poetry. Specializes in non-traditional styling and distribution of poetry and other creative wordsmithing. As a hobby Alex also does work with graphic and web design but dreads going into these professionally. He loves water, driving, and long walks on the beach.
